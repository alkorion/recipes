Ingredients:
- quesadilla cheese ideally ("Queso Duranguense")
- corn tortillas (~6") 
- serano peppers
- epazote herb
- salt

Instructions:
Prep:
 - wash peppers and epazote
1. Heat a traditional Mexican comal to medium-heat, and warm tortilla until soft
2. Take softened tortillas off the comal, fill with cheese, an epazote leaf and a few pepper slices, and salt to taste
3. Fold filled tortilla, and place back on the heated comal
4. Cook evenly on both sides until cheese is melted and both sides are lighty grilled

Serve with salsa and enjoy!
